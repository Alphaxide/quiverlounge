About Us

Welcome To Quiver Lounge Bar & Grill

Little Story

Quiver Lounge & Grill, renowned for its exceptional bar and grill experience, stands as a culinary masterpiece that marries the art of mixology with the sizzle of the grill. Across multiple branches, including Quiver Lounge Kenol, Quiver Lounge Thika Road, Quiver Lounge Kilimani, and Quiver Lounge Kitengela, we redefine dining and entertainment.

Each Quiver Lounge & Grill branch offers an inviting ambiance where guests can indulge in the finest of bar products and grill delicacies. Our mixologists craft exquisite cocktails, while our grill masters expertly prepare succulent Nyama Choma and an array of grilled dishes that tantalize the palate.

At Quiver Lounge Kenol, the heart of our bar offerings lies in its diversity. From classic cocktails to innovative concoctions, our bar menu caters to every taste. Meanwhile, the grill at this branch takes center stage, serving up mouthwatering Nyama Choma platters that celebrate the art of char-grilled perfection.

Quiver Lounge Thika Road embodies a unique blend of grill expertise and bar excellence. Our grill menu features an array of delectable choices, from marinated skewers to tender steaks. Complement your meal with a selection from our diverse range of premium spirits and cocktails.

In the heart of Kilimani, Quiver Lounge Kilimani offers an urban oasis where grill enthusiasts and cocktail aficionados unite. Dive into our grill menu, where culinary expertise meets fire-kissed perfection. Meanwhile, our bar tempts with handcrafted cocktails and a curated selection of wines and spirits.

Quiver Lounge Kitengela welcomes guests to a delightful fusion of bar sophistication and grill mastery. Sip on signature cocktails, wines, or premium spirits as you savor our tantalizing Nyama Choma and grill favorites, expertly prepared to cater to your discerning taste.

Across all our branches, Quiver Lounge & Grill invites you to embark on a gastronomic journey where bar products and grill specialties converge to create an unforgettable dining and entertainment experience. Join us today and discover the artistry of flavor and hospitality.
